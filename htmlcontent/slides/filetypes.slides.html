TESS Data Product Workflow

Onboard the spacecraft, TESS obtains data at a rapid cadence, and coadds the data. TESS identifies targets from a target list onboard the computer, and selects small pixel cut outs around those regions. The FFIs and pixel cut outs are downlinked. The FFIs are then sent to the archive (MAST) and pixel cut outs are processed into TPFs, and then LCFS.
