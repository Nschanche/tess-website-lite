What can TESS do?


Provide all-sky, space-based, long-duration (30+ days) precision photometry for tens of millions of sources.

Enable studies of exoplanet systems, single and multiple stars, supernovae, galaxies, Solar System objects.

No reserved targets, no restricted science areas and no proprietary period. All data is made available through MAST immediately after data processing and validation is complete.

What is the TESS GI program?


The primary purpose of the TESS General Investigator Program is to enhance and maximize the science return from TESS.

The program facilitates and supports both 2-minute and 20-second cadence observations with TESS and research undertaken with the Full-Frame Images.
